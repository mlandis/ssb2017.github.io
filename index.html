Society of Systematic Biologists 2017 Standalone Meeting

What: The 2nd standalone meeting of the Society of Systematic Biologists (SSB).

Where: Baton Rouge, LA (Hosted by Louisiana State University)

When: Jan. 8-10, 2017

Following up on the success of the SSB 2015 Standalone Meeting, we are organizing a meeting in Baton Rouge. It will feature workshops, debates, lightning talks, and more. Capacity is capped at 300 attendees. Stay tuned for more details about the meeting as it develops!
